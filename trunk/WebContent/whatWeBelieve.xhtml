What We Believe

One God, the God of Abraham, Moses, Jesus, and Muhammad, peace be upon them all.

All of the Holy Scriptures, i.e., the Scrolls of Abraham, those Psalms written by David, the Torah revealed to Prophet Moses, the Gospel given to Prophet Jesus, and the Qur’an revealed to Prophet Muhammad, as they appeared in their original forms.

All of the prophets listed in the Qur’an , of which at least 20 are also mentioned in either the Tanakh (Jewish scriptures) or the Bible, starting with Adam and ending with Muhammad, peace be upon them all.

All of the angels listed in the Tanakh, Bible, and the Qur’an.

Heaven and Hell, eternal life after death, and personal accountability on Judgment Day after resurrection.

Peaceful co-existence with people of ALL other faiths, by promoting personal understanding, interaction, and engagement in America and throughout the world.

That the acceptance of faith in a person’s heart is entirely between the person and his/her creator, God.

Conveying the message of and information about Islam, not to proselytize nor evangelize Islam.

Extending the maximum co-operation possible to inter-faith agencies who share the same values.
