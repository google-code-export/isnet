Mentor Certification Help

 

This is the most important task and trust the certified Mentor has. Other mentors who have just completed the Da’wah training, passed all of the tests and paid the Annual certification fee, send a request to you to certify them. It is imperative you meet them in person (or over video conferencing), verify their ID, ask them about their feelings and views about terrorism and killing of non-Muslims, etc. It is critical that we do not allow extremists to be part of the Myc2i network. MyC2i is designed for the moderate mainstream Muslim to interact with the non-Muslims and personally show them that we are peace loving honorable people. This module shows the list of mentors who are requesting your interview for certification and also want to be under your [lead] mentorship.

 

After the personal interview if you are satisfied with the interview [based on the criteria stated above], you can confirm the mentor as a certified mentor.

When you will click on the confirm certification button, the member will be certified and an email will be sent to the member about the confirmation of his/her certification. Finally the member will drop off from this list, since he/she is already certified.
