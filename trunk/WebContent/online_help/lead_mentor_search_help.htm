Lead Mentor Search Help

 

This feature works very similarly to the Protégé Search feature. When a mentor needs to find and contact a lead mentor to confirm his/her certification or for any other reason, this feature allows the search. After successful completion of the tutorial, tests, and payment the mentor can search for a nearby lead mentor to arrange a personal interview and approve certification. After the certification is confirmed by lead mentor, the certified mentor can search for protégés.

The search feature allows the mentor specify the zip code and some other filter criteria like profession, ethnicity, marital status, year of birth etc.
