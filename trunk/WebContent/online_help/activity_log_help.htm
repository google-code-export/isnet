Mentor Activity Log Help

 

The Mentor Activity log allows you to create some pre-defined activities like a coffee meeting request, Call back, Dinner meeting request, mentor request and protégé request etc.

 

The upper part shows the pending activities of the mentor and the lower part shows the old activities (history). Your lead mentor can view your activities to ensure you are looking after your protégés as you should be. You can also filter the history of activities by time like last quarter, last month, last week and today. You can also view the details of each activity or delete each item.

 

 

You can create a new activity by clicking on the 'create new activity' link. You have to put the receiver name, in the receiver fields where there is an auto-complete list. When you type something here then you will find a list of mentors or protégés related to you. In other words, only protégés who have accepted to be your protégé or mentors who have agreed to be your lead/follow mentor will appear in the list box. You need to select one or more of them. Then select the type of activity, and complete the subject line and body. Finally, when you press the send button, it will be sent to the destination member(s).
