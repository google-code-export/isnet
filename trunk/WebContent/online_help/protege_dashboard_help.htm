Guest/Protégé Dashboard

Guest and Protégé are used interchangeably in the MyC2i system.

Protégé dashboard is the default home page of the Protégé. After the successful login, the Protégé is taken to the dashboard.

The Protégé dashboard is logically divided into three sections. The left most section, the middle section and the right section.


1. Left section: The left section contains the navigation menu, story related sub sections. Each sub section is explain the following:

1.1 Top ten storeis section: The section contians the top ten stories and view points approved by the administrator of the site. When you submit a story or viewpoint then it should be approved by the admin, once the admin confirm the story of viewpoint then it is available on the dashboard.

1.2 Winning story section: Every week there is a story competion, submitted by the mentor. The most votted story is selected and displayed in this section.

1.3 Winning viewpoint section: Every week there is a viewpoint competion, submitted by the protege. The most votted viewpoint is selected and displayed in this section.

1.4 Sccess stories: The section all the stories submitted by all Protege/protege. Only five headline are displayed at a time. Clicking on each headline the details will be displayed in a popup window.

1.5 Browse more stories (Story Voting): After clicking the browse more stories user is forwarded to another page contains all the stories with facilty to vote each stories. Each user can vote one story

 


2. Middle section: The middle section contains the request from proteges or mentors. Each sub section is explain the following:

2.1 Pending activities : This section contains all the activies request coming from Mentor or protege. Like meeting request, visit request etc.

2.2 Mentor hierchy section: The section contains the Mentor of the protege. Or if the mentor of this protege has lead mentor then it shows all the mentors of this lead mentor.


3. Right section: The right section contains the Protege expiry date section, Protege to do list what's new section and important link section.

3.1 Whats' new section : This section contains rectent news posted by myc2i site.

3.2 Important links section: The section contains the list of important web site related to Islam

3.3 Common Islamic terms: This section contains the list common Islamic terms.
