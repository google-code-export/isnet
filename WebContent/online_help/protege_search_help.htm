Protégé Search Help

 

After the mentor is certified, the mentor may search for a protégé locally (using the ZIP criteria) or nationally. We recommend that you search for a protégé in your area which will facilitate personal face-to-face meetings and interaction. It is critical that you invite the non-Muslim to your home for a meal or coffee/tea. While searching for protégés you specify the zip code range where you would like to find the protégés.  You can also specify some other filter criteria like profession, ethnicity, marital status, year of birth so you best match your attributes with your protégé.
